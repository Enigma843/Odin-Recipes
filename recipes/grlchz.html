Grilled Cheeze

Ain Going Nowhere

Timeless Classic

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheeze
  • Butter

    Steps

  1. Heat Pan and add butter.
  2. Place desired amount of cheeze on bread.
  3. Cover bread and cheeze with other slice of bread.
  4. Add another slab of butter to the pan
  5. Flip Sandwhich
  6. Cook until golden brown.
  7. Enjoy!
